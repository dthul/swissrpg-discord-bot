Privacy statement

The access you grant to the bot Hyperion will only be used to give you access to the Discord channels you have registered to. Additionally, we will register you automatically on Meetup to follow-up sessions of campaigns you are registered to. We neither store, nor share any of your data. If you do not wish to grant this access to our bot Hyperion, please contact a SwissRPG organiser. By clicking the button above, you accept these terms.
